Welcome to Aeromodelling Club

We are a group of passionate students dedicated to pushing the boundaries of aerospace innovation. Specializing in radio-controlled aircraft and drones, we combine engineering excellence with creative problem-solving.


Established in 2012, our team has consistently excelled in national competitions while fostering a culture of technical exploration and hands-on learning.
